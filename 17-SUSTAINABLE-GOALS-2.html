WHAT ARE THE SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT GOALS?

The Sustainable Development Goals, adopted by the United Nations in 2015 are a universal call to action! They are designed to end poverty, hunger, discrimination and much more, to ensure that by 2030 all people enjoy peace and prosperity It is recognized that action in one area will affect outcomes in others, and that development must balance social, economic and environmental sustainability.
